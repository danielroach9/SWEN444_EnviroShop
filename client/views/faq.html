General questions

What is EnviroShop?

Enviroshop is an application built for consumers in mind. We allows shoppers to find local places that serve environmentally friendly foods.

Who created EnviropShop?

EnviroShop was created by enterpenuers who had healthy eating mind. They saw that people were frustrated with unhealthy fast foods places and found a simple solution to the complex problem of finding healthy meals to eat.

Where can I email questions to?

Please send your questions and concerns to CustomerSupport@enviroshop.com
Shoppers

What are reviews for?

Reviews are meant for shoppers to leave feedback for businesses they have been to and for products they have tried.

How do I find stores?

To find a store use our map feature with the icon above in the top bar. By clicking the map icon you will be taken to multiple stores around your location.

Why isn't a store I use on EnviroShop?

Businesses have the option to sign up for our application. If your favorite store/restaurant is not listed urge them to sign-up!
Businesses

What are product listings?

Product Listings are an oppurtinity for you as business to list what you have in store and market it to our user base.

How do I reply to a review?

To reply to reviews just look at the review you have been given and use the reply button listed next to it.
